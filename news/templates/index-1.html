From the very beginning

Stablished in 1956, Clark County School District is the fifth largest school district in the country, educating almost 75 percent of all students in Nevada with more than 320,000 students enrolled in Kindergarten through 12th grade.

Beliefs

Our emphasis is placed on excellence that seeks to instill a desire to keep learning, to be self-motivated and self-disciplined and to believe in one's self-worth.

  • The first of the educational process is the student.
  • The environment must be caring, healthy, safe.
  • The school is an integral part of the community.
  • All students are capable of learning.
  • Families must be actively involved in education.
  • Our communities must be active.

The members of the School District governing Board establish policy, hire personnel, develop annual budgets, and plan facilities needed to implement a successful public school program.
